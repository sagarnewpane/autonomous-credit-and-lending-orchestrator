# Compliance and Risk Metrics Calculation

This document outlines how the orchestration engine calculates and evaluates compliance, risk, and fraud indicators across the extracted documents and wallet transactions.

## Income Profile Metrics

### Average Monthly Primary Income (AMPI)
- **Definition:** The median monthly salary income (salary category only).
- **Why Median?** More robust to outliers such as bonuses or irregular month-end payments.

### Total Monthly Verified Income (TMVI)
- **Formula:** `AMPI + (Avg Secondary Income × 0.7)`
- **Secondary Income Averaging:** Only averages months where secondary income actually occurred (not zero-padded across entire history).
- **Risk Discount:** Secondary income is weighted at 70% to account for lower stability/verification compared to primary salary.
- **Interpretation:** The most conservative verified monthly capacity the borrower can demonstrate.

## 1. Compliance Checks (The `Compliance Agent`)
The compliance node provides advisory checks and document validations. It appends its findings to a `compliance_notes` list without immediately halting the pipeline.

### Cross-Document Consistency
- **Check:** Discrepancies in core identities across provided files (e.g., Citizenship Card vs. Lal Purja vs. Tax Clearance).
- **Trigger:** Looks for the `citizenship_mismatch_across_documents` flag generated by the Parser Agent or searches for multiple distinct citizenship IDs embedded in the parsed documents payload (`extracted_docs`).
- **Result:** Generates an advisory warning if differences are detected.

### Advisory Capacity Checks
- **Check:** Validates the applicant's Capacity Ratios against predefined thresholds.
- **Thresholds:** 
  - Generates a warning if **Debt-to-Income (DTI)** > 50%.
  - Generates a warning if **Loan-to-Income (LTI)** > 1.5x.

## 2. Risk and Fraud Indicators (The `Income Agent` & `Calculations Profile`)
These metrics evaluate quantitative risk combining both Document-claimed income and mathematically verified Wallet (Transaction) volume.

### Income Mismatch Ratio
**The Key Fraud Signal.** Tracks the discrepancy between the income claimed on official documents and the actual cash flow passing through connected wallet platforms.
- **Formula:** `Document Monthly Income ÷ Wallet Monthly Volume`
- **Wallet Volume Includes:** ALL CREDIT transactions through wallet platforms (eSewa, Khalti, FonePay), regardless of category. This captures the complete picture of money flowing through wallet apps to detect discrepancies.
- **Interpretation:** A ratio significantly higher than `1.0` highlights that document income exceeds verified wallet activity—a red flag for potential fraud.

### Income Stability Score
Evaluates the behavioral dependability of cash inflows out of **100 points**.
- **Formula:** `100 - (Volatility CV × 100)`
- **Bonus:** `+ 20 points` added if the income falls within a strict "Formal Employment" cadence window (28-31 day payment gaps). 
- **Limits:** Min `0`, Max `100`.

### Tax Trust Score
Measures actual tax compliance and payment authenticity (out of **100 points**).
- **Formula:** 
  - `+ 50 points` base score if a valid tax compliance document/flag is present.
  - `+ up to 50 points` based on the effective tax rate mathematically scaling with taxes paid (`min(50, effective_tax_rate × 100 × 5)`).
- **Interpretation:** Discriminates between applicants who simply "filed" taxes vs. those who genuinely paid a reasonable effective tax rate.

### Estimated Monthly Capacity
A conservative monthly income proxy used to price the loan safely.
- **Formula:** `Minimum(Document Monthly Income, Wallet Monthly Volume)`
- **Interpretation:** Ensures that high document income is not uniquely trusted if wallet transactions can't verify the liquidity.

### Capacity Ratios
Advisory ratios generated to evaluate lending capability.
- **Debt-To-Income (DTI):** 
  - `(Existing Monthly Liabilities + Estimated New Loan Monthly Payment) ÷ Estimated Monthly Capacity`
  - *Note:* Estimated New Payment assumes a baseline 36-month loan term `(Loan Amount / 36)`.
- **Loan-To-Income (LTI):**
  - `Loan Amount ÷ (Estimated Monthly Capacity × 12)`
